NOMINATION AND ENTRY

The nomination into the ATABA awards is open to all legally registered businesses & Individuals operating in Abia State.

VOTING PROCESS

Voting is open to the general public. You can only vote once in a day and within the applicable Voting Rules.

AWARD CEREMONY

The Award Dinner will take place in Abia, Nigeria. Attendance is strictly by invitation

ABOUT THE

AWARDS

ABIA Technology & Business Awards (ATABA) is an award organized to Celebrate and reward Technology Entrepreneurs, Innovators, Inventors, Academicians and policy makers (Government) in Nigeria. The objective is to promote and commend the excellent achievements to which Nigeria Technology professionals and organizations contribute towards building a sustainable economy using technology, the Awards also aimed to encourage local practitioners to develop innovative and creative Technology solutions, which will uplift the image of Nigeria both locally and internationally.
